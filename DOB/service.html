MATERNITY, BABY AND CHILD PHOTOGRAPHY IN PUNE

SERVICES AT MY PHOTO STUDIO

My artistic photo studio is located in the lush greenery of Bavdhan, Pune suburbs. It is thus, more warm and cozy than just being a fully equipped professional photography space. I strive to ensure comfort, safety, peace and privacy of all parents and children. I spend most of our time clicking magical photos in this carefully planned and personally designed atmosphere.

Keeping in mind that some of the sessions last longer, I selected the space that would be comfortable for all. The photo studio is featuring 2 rooms from which one is solely dedicated to parent’s for their short breaks, feeding the baby or changing clothes. The rest of the space is planned for various setups and has plenty of natural light. In the storage corners you will find an immense collection of props and required professional photography equipment.

I am the only international photographer in Pune, who exceptionally provides maternity and baby photography services. I click babies in 100% safe Profoto studio lighting by using 2 diffuser layers in huge soft-box to protect his/her sensitive eyes. Throughout my newborn sessions I use soothing baby sounds to save the sleep. I will never leave him/her unattended or in no circumstances try to lift or hang him/her with any kind of props. I carefully search for highest quality cloths (100% cotton, woolen, handmade, organic and soft), sturdy pieces of mostly international props. As well I incorporate my in-depth baby handling knowledge. That was gained through various workshops (by Kelly Brown, Erind Elizabeth, Amy Macdaniel and more). My support member is perfectly trained for the same.

WHAT I LOVE THE MOST!

Is to focus my lens on people and not to overshadow them with rainbows behind. Therefore, you will find my photos more candid, deep and vintage like with solo pieces of wooden barrels, swing, rustic rugs, flower fences and hand-painted canvas backgrounds. The view of beautiful garden in my clicks that I get from Pashan lake that is only a short distance away or other near by locations.

When it comes to creativity, I am inspired by natural surroundings and try to bring in the colors, backdrops, materials, furniture and ambience, reflecting the same. Using pastel colors in the backdrops and props is yet one more feature.

What is more, I work hand in hand with all parents, assuring their comfort of communication, attention to details and creative services. Based on that, I personally design the creative setups, click all the photos and edit it post the shoots.

Thinking of parents’ time and comfort, I constantly update variety of designer made gowns, baby clothes and pre-book make-up artist.
